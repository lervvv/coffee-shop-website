BUT FIRST, COFFEE

Coffee is one of the most popular beverages in the world, enjoyed by millions every day.

It is believed to have been discovered in Ethiopia around the 9th century when a goat herder noticed his goats becoming unusually energetic after eating berries from a certain tree. This led to the discovery of coffee beans. Today, coffee is not only a beloved drink but also a significant cultural and social element in many countries. It is known for its stimulating effects due to caffeine, which can help improve focus and alertness.
